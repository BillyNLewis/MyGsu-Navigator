About

Navigating an unfamiliar campus, especially one as large as Georgia State can be daunting at times. But fear not! MyGsu Navigator is developed to help students find their way around Georgia State University. You can rely on MyGsu Navigator to provide accurate step-by-step directions to classroom buildings, recreation centers, libraries, housing, and dining halls from your current location or another point on campus.
